Standup

Morning standup with design and engineering. Keep the team focused and brief! Update active cards, prioritize the backlog, assign work. Share stakeholder feedback.

Analyze and organize your teams' data. Are your teams/customers headed in the right direction? Make time to think critically about whats going well and what isn't.
